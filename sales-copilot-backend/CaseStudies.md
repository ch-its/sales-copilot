# B2B Sales Case Studies

## Scenario 1: The Legacy Migration
- **Persona**: Sarah, VP of Sales
- **Context**: Customer uses a 15-year-old on-premise CRM. Terrified of data loss.
- **Starting Hook**: Look, I'm busy, and I highly doubt you have anything new for me.

## Scenario 2: The CFO's Wall
- **Persona**: Mark, CFO
- **Context**: Absolute hiring and software spending freeze for 6 months.
- **Starting Hook**: Unless this pays for itself by Friday, we aren't buying.

## Scenario 3: The Compliance Gatekeeper
- **Persona**: David, CTO (Healthcare)
- **Context**: Strict HIPAA compliance. Skeptical of AI training on patient data.
- **Starting Hook**: How do I know our patient data isn't training your models?
